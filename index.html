Wings Ocean Heartbeat Project.

We are a dedicated organization with a primary mission to assist children with heart conditions. At Wings Ocean Heartbeat Project, we not only provide financial support for heart treatments for these children but also perform heart surgeries free of charge. We take pride in being a crucial part of healing and bringing hope to these children and their families.

WHY
WE DO IT

In 2012, one of Ms. Selena's nieces, who is the CEO of our company, was diagnosed with congenital heart disease at just 7 days old. She had to live in the hospital's emergency room until the seventh month of her life.

During the time she cared for her niece, every night she witnessed at least one child passing away for various reasons. The pain deeply affected everyone who witnessed it, and fortunately, her niece's life was saved thanks to the hospital's heart surgery fund.

Since that day, a profound sense of gratitude has grown within her, motivating her to support the next generation by sponsoring heart surgeries for underprivileged children.

HOW
WE DO IT

We support children by utilizing a portion of the profits from our company to give back to the community.
This includes performing free heart surgeries for them as well as providing medication and treatment. We are committed to ensuring that no child is left behind due to heart disease.

When you engage with our business endeavors, you're not just investing in a product or service; you're contributing to a cause that touches the lives of children and their families.
